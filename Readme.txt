== Description ==

Embed your social media feeds, hashtag feeds, or geofenced media feeds on your Wordpress blog using Ampsy for Wordpress.

Overview of Ampsy for Wordpress:

1. Social Page

Get started with your FREE Social Page. The Social Page tool enables you to quickly connect your social account(s). Our system then creates a feed that pulls all of your social posts into one spot. It's a great way to keep your audience up-to-date on all of your social activity.

2. Campaigns

Ampsy enables you to create various types of social aggregation campaigns. When you upgrade to any of our paid plans, you can create:

= Social Hubs =

With this tool, you can aggregate social content based on hashtag, user handles, and @mentions across the social sphere.

= Crowdbooks =

With this tool, you can create a "geofence" and aggregate social content shared within a radius of any location in the world! Just enter an address, radius, and start/stop date & time and watch your feed populate with social content shared at that exact location.

3. Widgets

The widgets section enables to create short codes for any of your campaigns. Paste your short code(s) in the posts or pages you'd like to see them displayed

4. Subscriptions

We have several subscription options that give you additional campaign limits and import options.

== Installation ==

1. Extract the contents of the zip file into your wp-content/plugins directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Find the Ampsy Widgets icon in the bottom left of the sidebar dashboard
4. Follow the instructions in the Welcome Screen to create campaigns & widgets
